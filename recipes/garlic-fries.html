Garlic Fries Recipe



Descreption:

This is a recipe for preparing frozen fries, and then creating a delicious garlic based coating to really amp them up!


Ingredients:

  • One 32 ounce bag of french fries potatoes (shoestring)
  • 2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp fresh, chopped garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh, finely chopped parsely
  • 2 tsp kosher salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven according to package instructions on frozen french fries. Line a cookie sheet with foil.
  2. Place frozen fries onto cookie sheet, spread them out in a single layer and place into hot oven.
  3. Pour oil into a mason jar. Chop the garlic into small pieces and add to olive oil in jar. Place lid on jar and shake to mix up.
  4. Chop parsley into small pieces and set aside.
  5. Take cooked fries from oven, scoop into a bowl. Drizzle garlic oil over the hot fries, tossing to coat evenly. Sprinkle salt and parsley over the fries and toss again and then EAT! Enjoy friends!
